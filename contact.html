Common questions

Before you reach out — quick answers

Are you based in Australia?

I'm based in India but work exclusively with Australian clients. I'm available during AEST business hours for calls, and all my content is written specifically for the Australian market — local references, Australian English and all.

How fast do you turn around content?

Standard turnaround is 48–72 hours for blog posts and social content. Landing page rewrites take 5–7 business days. Rush delivery is available — just mention it in your enquiry.

Do you offer long-term contracts?

No lock-in contracts. I work month-to-month — if you're not happy, you can stop at any time. My goal is to earn your business every month through results, not contracts.

Do you offer a free trial?

Each service has a free offer — a free audit, a free page review or free sample memes — so you can see the quality of my work before spending a cent. Check the individual service pages.

Do you invoice in AUD?

Yes — all pricing is quoted and invoiced in Australian dollars. I accept bank transfer and PayPal. Payment terms are 7 days from invoice.

What industries do you specialise in?

I work across SaaS, fintech, real estate, eCommerce, professional services and hospitality. If your industry isn't listed, reach out anyway — I've likely written for something similar.

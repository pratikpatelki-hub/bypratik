A content writer who thinks like a marketer

I started writing content because I genuinely love the craft — but what kept me going was seeing how the right words can completely transform a business's results. A well-written blog that ranks. A landing page that converts. A social post that gets shared. That's what I'm here for.

I specialise in working with Australian SMEs — from Melbourne SaaS startups to Sydney real estate agencies to Brisbane eCommerce brands. I understand the Australian market, the tone Australian readers respond to, and the cultural nuances that make content feel local rather than generic.

Beyond writing, I stay sharp on the latest in SEO, AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation) and GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation) — because Australian businesses need to be found not just on Google, but on ChatGPT, Perplexity and whatever comes next.

When I'm not writing, I'm testing new content formats, studying conversion rate optimisation, and keeping up with how Australian audiences are consuming content online.

Three things I believe about good content

These aren't just values — they're the principles that drive every piece of writing I produce for Australian clients.

Clarity over cleverness

Australian readers value directness. I write content that's easy to read, easy to share and impossible to misunderstand — no fluff, no jargon.

📊

Strategy before words

Before I write a single sentence, I research your audience, competitors and keywords. Good content starts with good data.

Partnership, not transactions

I want to understand your business deeply. The best results come from long-term relationships where I know your brand voice inside and out.

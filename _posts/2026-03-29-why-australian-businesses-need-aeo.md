Search is no longer only ten blue links. Buyers now ask AI tools for direct recommendations and compare answers quickly.

For Australian businesses, that means your content needs to be:

- clear and specific
- built around real customer questions
- supported by proof, examples, and consistent messaging

## A practical starting framework

1. Build pages around one intent each.
2. Add short, direct answers before long explanations.
3. Use local context such as city names, pricing range, and use cases.
4. Refresh high-value pages every month.

## What to track

Monitor branded search growth, qualified lead quality, and assisted conversions from educational content pages.

Posting time impacts early engagement, and early engagement influences total reach.

For most Australian B2B pages, strong windows are:

- Tuesday to Thursday
- 8:00 to 9:30 AEST
- 12:00 to 1:00 AEST
- 4:30 to 6:00 AEST

## Improve consistency before chasing virality

Start with three posts per week for six weeks. Keep hook quality and visual style consistent so the algorithm can learn your audience match.

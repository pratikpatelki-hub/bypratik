Most pages do not fail because of design polish. They fail because visitors cannot answer three questions quickly:

- Is this for me?
- Why should I trust this?
- What should I do next?

## Quick fix sequence

1. Rewrite the headline with a concrete outcome.
2. Add proof near the first call-to-action.
3. Remove extra navigation from campaign landing pages.
4. Tighten form fields to only what sales truly needs.
